Julia Xu is a user experience designer with an affinity for adventurous variable rewards. She finds the delicate blend of creativity, strategy, and muscle memory of UX design to be endlessly captivating.

She grew up in Maryland, completed college in Abu Dhabi, and currently lives in San Francisco. She has also whipped her hair back and forth around Accra (west Africa), NYC, Buenos Aires, Prague, Shanghai, London, and over 15 countries.

In her free time, she enjoys going to sci-fi book clubs, meeting new people (like you!), wrestling with video games, and writing stories, fictional and not.
